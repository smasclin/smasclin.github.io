AI Apps & Interactive Games

Shared Tools

Custom apps and interactive games built for ESS students at UC Merced — AI tutors, climate simulators, sorting challenges, and review games. Free to use, no account required.

All tools open in a new tab. Most run directly in your browser with no login needed. Tools built on Playlab.ai use conversational AI; the others are standalone web apps.

🤖
T-ESS 001 · Tutoring Tool Tutoring ESS001 An AI-powered tutoring assistant loaded exclusively with ESS001 (Introduction to Environmental Science) course content, supporting student learning through concept review, mock tests, study guides, and review games. Available 24/7! Open tool
🎮
ESS002 · Review Activity ESS002 Jeopardy — Module 1 A Jeopardy-style game to review the first four chapters of the course — sustainability science, life on Earth, ecosystems, and human population — and prepare for the exam. Open tool
🌍
Role-Play Activity Climate Dialogue Simulator Play the role of a climate change supporter or denier and engage in a conversation with the opposite side to practice critical thinking and argumentation. Open tool
🎙️
Role-Play Activity Interview of an Environmentalist Interview a major current or past environmentalist figure through a configured chatbot to explore key moments in environmentalism history. Open tool
♻️
Interactive Game Sort It Right Based on Merced County waste management guidelines, your goal is to drag and drop 20 everyday items into the correct bin — grey (landfill), blue (recycling), or green (organics). Open tool
☁️
Interactive Game Cloud Identification Identify clouds in 16 different photographs. A cloud chart is always available to guide you — so you can sharpen your atmospheric science skills without the pressure. Open tool
🌡️
Interactive Game Your World, Your Climate Take a short quiz about your lifestyle and discover how much warming would occur if everyone shared your habits. Learn about the environmental consequences — and about the changes that make a real difference. Open tool
